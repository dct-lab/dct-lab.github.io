The Data Center Security Research Team (DCT Lab @IIE-CAS, Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences) focuses on data center security requirements and conducts systematic research at the network, system, and information levels, focusing on Virtulization OS, Cloud Security, and Software Defined Perimeter.
